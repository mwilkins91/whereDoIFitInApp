Life Expectancy

By looking at the life expectancy for the average male in Canada, and the health statistics for your age group, we have generated a countdown timer.

Scroll down to view how much longer you are expected to live, and then take a look at the countdown for your comparison country.

Notice that if you lived in the USA you would lose an estimated 1 year(s), 2 day(s), 3 hour(s), and 4 minute(s) of your life.
